About IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) was established in compliance with NAAC guidelines to develop a system for conscious, consistent, and catalytic improvement in the overall performance of our institution. The IQAC aims to promote a quality culture through institutionalizing best practices in teaching, learning, research, and administration.

Vision

To establish a quality culture that permeates all aspects of institutional functioning and ensures continuous improvement in academic and administrative processes.

Mission

To facilitate quality enhancement through systematic planning, implementation, and monitoring of quality initiatives while ensuring stakeholder satisfaction and institutional excellence.

Functions & Objectives

Quality Benchmarks

Development and application of quality benchmarks/parameters for various academic and administrative activities of the institution.

Documentation

Facilitating the creation of a learner-centric environment conducive to quality education and faculty maturation to adopt teaching methodologies.

Feedback System

Collection and analysis of feedback from all stakeholders on quality-related institutional processes and disseminating the same to concerned bodies.

AQAR Preparation

Preparation of the Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) to be submitted to NAAC based on the quality parameters and timelines.

Quality Culture

Development of quality culture in the institution through organizing workshops, seminars, and quality-related awareness programs.

Continuous Improvement

Acting as a nodal agency for coordinating quality-related activities and ensuring the enhancement and integration of various measures.

NAAC Seven Criteria

01 Curricular Aspects

Focus: Curriculum design, development, and planning

  • Academic flexibility and choice-based credit system
  • Curriculum enrichment and updating
  • Feedback mechanisms for curriculum development
  • Integration of cross-cutting issues in curriculum

02 Teaching-Learning and Evaluation

Focus: Student-centric teaching-learning process

  • Student enrollment and profile
  • Catering to student diversity
  • Teaching-learning process
  • Teacher profile and quality
  • Evaluation process and reforms
  • Student performance and learning outcomes

03 Research, Innovations and Extension

Focus: Promotion of research culture and outreach activities

  • Research promotion and support
  • Resource mobilization for research
  • Innovation ecosystem
  • Research publications and awards
  • Extension activities and community engagement

04 Infrastructure and Learning Resources

Focus: Physical and academic support facilities

  • Physical facilities
  • Library as learning resource
  • IT infrastructure and e-governance
  • Maintenance of campus infrastructure

05 Student Support and Progression

Focus: Student support services and career guidance

  • Student mentoring and support
  • Student diversity and inclusion
  • Student activities and progression
  • Alumni engagement

06 Governance, Leadership and Management

Focus: Institutional governance and leadership

  • Institutional vision and leadership
  • Strategy development and deployment
  • Faculty empowerment strategies
  • Financial management and resource mobilization

07 Institutional Values and Best Practices

Focus: Environmental consciousness and values

  • Institutional values and social responsibilities
  • Best practices
  • Institutional distinctiveness
