College Overview

Sethupathy Government Arts College is a prestigious co-educational institution established in 1965 in Ramanathapuram, Tamil Nadu. Named after the illustrious Sethupathi Kings, who were known for their contributions to education and social development in Tamil Nadu, the college has been serving the educational needs of the region for over six decades.

Affiliated with Alagappa University, Karaikudi, the college is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited by NAAC. The institution was established with the generous efforts of Raja of Ramanathapuram Sri R. Shanmuga Rajeswara Sethupathy to provide qualitative higher education for economically disadvantaged people of Ramanathapuram and its nearby areas.
