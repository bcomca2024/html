Course Structure & Credit System

All undergraduate programs follow a semester system with Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) as per University Grants Commission guidelines.

Semester System

  • Duration: 6 Semesters (3 Years)
  • Each Semester: 4-5 months
  • Credits per Semester: 20-25 credits
  • Total Credits: 120 credits minimum

Course Components

Core Courses

Major subject courses (50-55%)

Elective Courses

Subject-specific electives (20-25%)
