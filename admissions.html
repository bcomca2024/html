Admission Requirements

Academic Qualifications

  • 12th Standard Certificate from recognized board
  • Minimum 50% marks for Science programs
  • Subject-specific eligibility criteria
  • Valid Transfer Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)

Required Documents

  • 10th & 12th Standard Mark Sheets
  • Transfer & Character Certificates
  • Passport size photographs (6 nos.)
  • Aadhar Card copy
  • Community Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (for fee concession)

Fee Structure

  • Arts Programs: ₹5,000 per year
  • Science Programs: ₹8,000 per year
  • Commerce Programs: ₹6,000 per year
  • Special Fees: Laboratory & Library
  • Hostel Accommodation: Available
  • Scholarship opportunities available

Reservation Policy

  • SC/ST: 35% reservation
  • BC/MBC: 30% reservation
  • Differently Abled: 3% reservation
  • Sports Quota: 2% reservation
  • Ex-Servicemen: 2% reservation
  • First Generation Learners: Priority
